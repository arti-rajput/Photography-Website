About Me

I am a photographer who initially started photography as a side hobby and used to do gigs for free in friends and family circuit. As time flew, I realized how much I enjoy capturing moments and finally decided to adopt photography as my full time profession. Having said that, I am really grateful for each and every experience that life has had to offer me and I'm honestly humbled to be part of each and every project I have documented so far. It is crazy when you really stop and think about how many people have shown faith in me to be part of their special days. While I take this reponsibility very seriously and do strive to continue to work passionately with the clients in a comfortable and relaxed atmosphere, so you are free to be you.

My Services

After all the photography adventures over the last few years with experience of shooting in different venues and destinations all over the world, I offer a handful of photography services to meet all types of personal and business needs. The packages I provide are carefully planned so that your event goes off without a hitch!

If you spend some time to check the photo galleries, you will get a grasp of my skill of bringing life into even the simplest of subjects. My effort goes in capturing the true nature of the animate or inanimate objects and then retouching the images to uncloak their inner aesthetic.
